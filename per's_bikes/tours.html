Tours



San Francisco to Sausalito Classic Ride

9 miles/15 km, level 2, 3 hours. $39 + bike rent.


This classic bike ride shows our guests the best the Bay Area has to offer: Panoramic views of the Northern Californian landscape, the Golden Gate Bridge, and an optional ferry ride back to the City.


This tour is ideal for those who would like to see the beauty of the Bay Area while enjoying a fun bike ride with superb views of the Bay. And you’ll be biking on the world famous Golden Gate Bridge!


Our group sizes are at most 10 people, so we are able to ride at a relaxed pace with many stops to enjoy the uniqueness of the San Francisco Bay Area. The ride includes 3 moderate climbs.


If you want to experience all this, but you don't feel as though you can physically manage the ride, we suggest that you rent an electrical bicycle that will make the uphills a breeze!


Option:

For those who want to bike more, this ride can be extended to Tiburon. This option adds another 10 miles/16 km to the tour for only $20. There is a ferry from Tiburon that will bring us back to SF Pier 41. It’s a great option if you have more energy and you want to see more of the country side.


Detailed Description of San Francisco to Sausalito Tour

• We leave at 12 noon from GGB Rental, See map. Please be here around 11:40 AM for bike fitting.

• Briefing on the handling of the bike, overview of bike safety and the route for the day.

• We then begin our ride, making frequent stops as needed for hydration and photos. Remember, it’s a ride, not a race!

• Around 3 PM we arrive in Sausalito.

• The rest of the day is spent as you wish. For a $4 fee, you can return the bike near the ferry terminal in SF instead of the Haight St. shop.




Lazy day in the Park

8 miles/13 km, level 1, 2 hours. $29 + bike rent.


This is the bicycle tour for those who want to enjoy biking in San Francisco while exerting a minimal amount of effort. The ride begins and ends in the Haight neighborhood, located across from Golden Gate Park.


We go for a loop in Golden Gate Park, all the way out to Ocean Beach, where the brave ones can go for a dip in the waves! On this tour we’ll ride by the Conservatory of Flowers, The Bison paddock, the DeYoung museum, the Academy of Sciences, Stow Lake, the windmills, and more. The tour offers some spectacular views of the Pacific Ocean and interesting and fun facts about this great park.


Detailed Description of Lazy Day in the Park Tour

• We leave at 12 noon from GGB Rental, See map. Please be here around 11:40 AM for bike fitting.

• Briefing on the handling of the bike, overview of bike safety and the route for the day.

• We then begin our ride, making frequent stops as needed for hydration and photos. Remember, it’s a ride, not a race!

• We usually come back to the shop at around 2 PM. However, feel free to use the bike the whole day if you so wish. We’ll be more than happy to point out fun places to go in the neighborhood!




Marin Headlands Tour

22 miles/37 km, level 3, 5 hours. $49 + bike rent.


Here’s a tour that will make you break a sweat! Still, we will take plenty of breaks to take in the scenery. We’ll cross Golden Gate Bridge and then head up the very steep road to Hawk Hill. Have you ever wondered where those scenic bridge & city shots of San Francisco are taken? Wonder no more, you are here! A great descent takes us down to Point Bonita lighthouse where we sit down to enjoy a packed lunch.


After lunch we head back across the GGB and back to the shop.


Detailed Description of Marin Headlands Tour

• We leave at 11 AM from GGB Rental, See map. Please be here around 10:40 AM for bike fitting.

• Briefing on the handling of the bike, overview of bike safety and the route for the day.

• We then begin our ride, making frequent stops as needed for hydration and photos. Remember, it’s a ride, not a race!

• We usually come back to the shop at around 4 PM. However, feel free to use the bike the whole day if you so wish. We’ll be more than happy to point out fun places to go in the neighborhood!

• The bike has to be returned to the shop by 7 PM.
